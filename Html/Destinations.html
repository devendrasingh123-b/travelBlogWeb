1. Kashmir, India : "Heaven on Earth"
Why visit? Snow-capped mountains, Dal Lake, tulip gardens, and shikara rides. Best time: March to October Highlight: Gulmarg (for snow adventure), Pahalgam, and Sonamarg.
2. Kyoto, Japan : For Culture & Cherry Blossoms
Why visit? Traditional temples, geisha culture, and serene gardens. Best time: March:April (cherry blossom) or October:November (autumn) Highlight: Fushimi Inari Shrine, Arashiyama Bamboo Forest.
